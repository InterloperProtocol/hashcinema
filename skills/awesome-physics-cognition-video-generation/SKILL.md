# Awesome Physics Cognition Based Video Generation

Use the cloned repository at `external/awesome-physics-cognition-video-generation` as the survey map for physics-cognition video research.

## Use this skill when

- Building prompt rules around physical plausibility, motion, or interaction.
- Mapping the research space for text-to-video, video-to-video, 3D/4D generation, simulators, or world models.
- Collecting citations for a physics-aware video generation knowledge base.

## Work from the survey

- Inspect `external/awesome-physics-cognition-video-generation/README.md` for the taxonomy and paper list.
- Use the survey to separate schematic perception, passive cognition, active world simulation, and benchmark themes.
- Prefer the survey when choosing constraints, tags, or evaluation language for physically grounded video features.

## Practical guidance

- Treat it as a research index, not an implementation repo.
- Pull specific papers or benchmark families from the survey only when needed for the current prompt or feature.
- Use it to ground continuity language in real physics/video-generation categories.
